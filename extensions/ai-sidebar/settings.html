OpenBook Assistant settings

The assistant is off by default. No provider is configured, no network requests are made, and no telemetry is collected until you enable it here. The assistant is read-only and never takes actions without a separate per-action confirmation.

Local provider: requests go only to your Ollama daemon on this machine. Nothing is sent to any third party.

Data egress: with a bring-your-own-key provider, your question and any page/selection context you include are sent to the endpoint you configured, under that operator's terms. OpenBook ships no key and no provider; this leaves your device.
